Do I need any prior marketing experience?

▼
No! This program starts with the absolute basics in Week 1 — what digital marketing is, brand identity, and your first image creative. You'll build up progressively over 16 weeks.

What tools will I learn?

▼
You'll master Canva, CapCut, Adobe Express for creatives; Google Sheets for tracking; Botcake and Chatfuel for chatbot marketing; Pancake for CRM and orders; and Meta Ads Manager for paid campaigns.

How is the program structured?

▼
4 months, each with 4 weekly modules. Month 1: Creatives (Image & Video), Month 2: Creatives+ (Reinforcement), Month 3: Tools & Tech Platforms, Month 4: Ads Manager. Each week has 4 structured lessons with hands-on exercises.

Will I actually run real ad campaigns?

▼
Yes! In Month 4, Week 4 you'll plan, build, launch, and optimize a full Meta Ads campaign with real targeting, budgets, and performance reporting. You'll learn to read metrics like CPM, CPC, CTR, and ROAS.
